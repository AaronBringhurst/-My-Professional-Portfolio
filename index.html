About Me

With a dynamic 16-year tenure at Costco, a Fortune 100 behemoth, I have cultivated a multifaceted expertise in retail management, team leadership, and operational efficiency. My journey through various leadership roles—from team lead and supervisor to management positions—has afforded me a profound understanding of what it takes to drive success within a complex organizational structure. Known among my peers as an authority in warehouse operations, my contributions have been pivotal in transforming departments into cohesive, high-performing teams, thereby elevating their profitability and operational standards.

My proficiency is not limited to my knowledge and leadership skills; I am also recognized for my exceptional work ethic and productivity, particularly in forklift operations where I rank in the top 1% company-wide. This distinction is a testament to my dedication to excellence and my ability to significantly impact any department I am involved with, especially in turning around underperforming units with strategic problem-solving and management guidance.

Beyond my professional achievements, I am at a juncture where I seek to channel my drive, expertise, and passion into a new career path. This decision stems from a desire to find an environment where my contributions are not just recognized but rewarded, aligning more closely with my core values and professional aspirations.

My passion extends beyond my career; I am an aviation enthusiast with deep admiration for all things mechanical and the thrill of flight, a testament to my detail-oriented and high-stakes nature. Additionally, my entrepreneurial spirit led me to create Vertical Mile Rally, reflecting my love for unique automobiles and mechanical innovation. My dedication to excellence is mirrored in my personal pursuits as well, from dramatically improving my golf handicap through rigorous practice to embracing the vast universes within video games across genres.

As I pivot towards a new chapter in my professional journey, I bring with me a rich tapestry of skills, experiences, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. I am eager to leverage my background in operational leadership, team development, and my passion for innovation in a new setting where I can continue to drive success, inspire teams, and achieve personal and professional growth.
